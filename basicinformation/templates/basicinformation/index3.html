Example Use of BodhiAI

“Suppose, Rohan from MNIT comes to Bodhi AI platform and takes assessment on Newton’s Law of Motion that was taught in the class. Our platform will analyze his weak areas and other crucial performance indicators. Then the role of AI comes into play in the sense that it will predict Rohan’s future performance and can tell the areas where he is going to be weak in future. It will not only predict future performance, but will also recommend inter-connected videos and a series of smart tests. Then AI will update its own understanding of Rohan and will continue to bring in more relevant content and this process continues until Rohan is able to achieve best possible outcome. Thus AI is designed to create most efficient learning path for every student based on their performance, and that in itself acts as a personal assistant to every student”.
